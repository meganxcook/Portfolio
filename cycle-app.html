Cycle.

An inclusive, customizable menstrual cycle tracking app for menstruating people.

About

Cycle. is an inclusive, evidence-based menstrual cycle tracking app for all menstruating people. It was designed specifically for queer, trans, non-binary, gender-fluid, and gender non-conforming people to track their cycles. This app can help you get pregnant, avoid pregnancy, or gain awareness around your cycle patterns and menstrual health in a customizable and judgement-free way.
